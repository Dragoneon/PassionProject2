Hello there! As the indication states, I am Daniel. Born and raised in the States, American food is a favorite, and anything from fast-food restaurants to buffets appeal to me. From young age, video games were always addicting, with my first being a pixelated adventure and now a widely popular MOBA, League of Legends. Science and math have been my strong subjects, but the education system needs refinement and does not measure a person’s skill. There are plenty of other strengths people can have, for a fish can not be measured on how high it can climb a tree. After school, I usually go on my laptop and learn coding languages or wander in some of the quantum and physical sciences. I believe that cryptocurrencies will become the future way of trading, and have invested in Bitcoin and Litecoin. Seeking new innovations or understanding why something works is an enjoyable pastime (at least for me). I also browse the news and Reddit, looking at memes and current events. The overall goal of life is to contribute to society, and to do so, courage and self-determination are needed. For Ralph Waldo Emerson once proclaimed, “Do not go where the path may lead, go instead where there is no path and leave a trail.”

*If you question why I put my face on the internet, I ask, why do you let the government track your every step and make your information so vulnerable a simple attack by an advanced hacker can steal all of it, from your Social Security Number to your parent’s credit card?
