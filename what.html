INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Take any handheld device with dc motor and dismantle it, using it as the Farewll-Fly's mechanism. In this case, we took a fan motor.
  2. Inside, take the dc motor, electronics, and batteries (because who doesn't like a pair of batteries).
  3. After, cutt the top off of the device if neccessary and 3D print a custom cover for it in replacement, which may vary.
  4. If the wires were to come apart, solder them back together.
  5. Create the base, by 3D printing depending on size and shape. Generally, it would be a square pyramid base.
  6. Finally, create the tip, a long stick with the midpoint in the center of the motor cover with string on both sides for extra length.
  7. There are almost an infinite amount of add-ons you can do, from fidget spinner tops to suction cups on the bottom of the foundation.

As for the product, we do not intend to sell any, but offer as free information to let others try.
